Admissions Information

Admission Guidelines

  1. Application forms are available from the school office or downloadable below
  2. Submit completed forms with required documents
  3. Successful applicants will receive admission letters
  4. Pay required fees to secure placement
  5. Attend orientation before term begins

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Copy of last level results slip
  • Two recent passport photos
  • Medical report form (provided)
